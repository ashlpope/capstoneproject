ABOUT

This duo of cult cinema lovers regularly hosts theater screenings of your favorite action and horror classics (recent showings include Mad Max, The Thing, and Fire Walk With Me), alongside video store pop-ups and home media sale/swap events. For media collectors, there’s no better local group to be a part of.
We are a movie screening business devoted to bringing the most unique movie experiences to the masses. Follow along with us if you enjoy the cinema experience while we take over one venue after another for the sake of cinephiles everywhere and show you things the mega-movie houses won’t.
